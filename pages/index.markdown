## intro

hey, you found my website. welcome!

you may be wondering why all of the text on this site is in lowercase. some of that is rooted in my [software engineering background][resume] where i was taught to use [camelCase][camelCase-info] or [snake_case][snake_case-info]. but i also just kind of like the way it looks. i want this site to be a comforting place and i think lowercase lettering conveys a sense of ease and relaxation.

anyways, on to the burning question you came to this wesbite for: who is owen stegall? that's me!

all joking aside, i was born in statesville, north carolina in 2000 but i call conover, north carolina my home after i moved there in the summer of 2013. i graduated from newton-conover high school in 2018 and the university of north carolina at chapel hill in 2022. i entered the job market as a software engineer and worked in that field for nearly three years. however, i recently chose to transition into a legal career by pursuing a juris doctor at the university of south carolina where i expect to graduate from in 2028.

this website is meant to be a collection of information about me. so if you're here to consider hiring me, you can check out my [resume here][resume]. i'm also going to link to all of my [old essays][old-essays], [law school notes][law-school-notes], and [code respositories][code] here so i don't have to store them on my desktop anymore

i also have a ton of new and ongoing [hobbies][current-hobbies], as well as thoughts on the [books][current-books], [shows][current-tv-shows], and [movies][current-movies] i'm currently watching! i might use this site as a [place to write down some thoughts][blogs] from time to time.
